About us


xaashi.com is the leading classifieds website for users in the East Africa. Since its launch in 2015, xaashi.com has become the number one platform for users to buy, sell, or find anything in their community.
A community where underused goods are redistributed to fill a new need, and become wanted again, where non- product assets such as space, skills and money are exchanged and traded in new ways that do not always require centralized institutions or middlemen.
